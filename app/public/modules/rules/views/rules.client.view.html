Rules

Core rules

  • 3 to 6 players wants to conquer Lyon.
  • They have 1 week to show their power.
  • The winner is the one with more points at the end.

Actions

  • Units can travel by car, metro, bike or walking.
  • A player can attack a border zone with a chosen number of units.
  • An attack requires travel and at least one unit must stay in the departure zone.
  • Units can be bought and sold using the local money : the Gonette.
  • A unit can be put in regeneration mode. In this mode, it can't attack but it can still defend.

Attacks

  • The winner is set depending on the number and the abilities of the fighting units.
  • The winner becomes the zone's master and gain 1 point for its victory as well as 10 Gonettes.
  • The loser looses all its special units that fought.
  • The loser keeps half of its fighting Lyonnais and and allocates them to other zones.

Points

  • A victory gives 1 point.
  • Each zone under control at the end gives 1 point.
  • A bonus of 15 points is gained when a goal is reached.
  • Another bonus of 15 points is gained if the goal is still achieved at the end.

Units

  • Bikers : biking really fast !
  • Students : powerful but can't travel by day (in class) nor thursday nights (partying).
  • Hippies : don't pay tax to malls, got higher defense in parks.
  • Joggers : walking really fast !
  • Doctors : very good in defense and can heal.
  • Preasts : can convert one adverse unit during attack.
  • Scientists : really smart during fights !

Money

  • Banks create 2 Gonettes each 6th hour.
  • Malls tax 2 Gonnette from each border zone each 6th hour.
  • A victory gives 10 Gonettes.
  • A defeat gives 5 Gonettes.
  • Each type of unit has a price in Gonettes.
